GitHub Contributions

Hey, I'm Aaron Simfukwe — a Computer Science student at the University of Zambia with a genuine obsession for AI and web development. I first fell in love with coding when I realised that a few lines of text could create something people actually use. That feeling never left me.

My biggest current interest is the intersection of Large Language Models and practical web applications. I believe AI is not just a tool — it is a new programming primitive that changes how we build everything. I spend a lot of time on GitHub reading great code, contributing to open-source projects, and pushing my own experiments live.

I am also deeply excited about building for Zambia's growing digital economy — integrating local payment systems like Airtel Money and MTN MoMo, building mobile-first apps for low-bandwidth environments, and helping small businesses get online. I want to be part of the generation that puts Zambia on the global tech map.

When I am not coding, you will find me reading AI research papers, tweaking my GitHub profile, or thinking about how to turn a side project into a real startup. I am always open to collaborating on something interesting.
